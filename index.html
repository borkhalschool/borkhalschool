A Few Introductory Words from the Desk of Principal Border Guard Public School & College, Sylhet, an ideal educational institution, directly patronized by BGB Sylhet sector is situated in the holy city of Sylhet at BGB Sector Akhalia, Sylhet. It is an untarnished institution which is secured, tuneless, covered with green trees and charm. It is a unique institution of acquiring proper knowledge and advanced teaching method which build up ideal human beings. It started its journey in the ocean of education in the year of 1993 after the name of its patronizing organization, Bangladesh Rifles High School, Sylhet and recognized as a secondary school in 1996. First batch appeared at SSC in 1997. On the way of its on going march, the institution upgraded as an intermediate college in 2012 maintaining all formalities set by Ministry of Education , BISE Sylhet, and it gets fullest moon by appearing its first batch at HSC Exam in 2014. It is regarded as one of the best institutions due to its discipline, teaching technique and favourable teaching environment. In the meantime BGPSC has achieved name and fame due to cent percent successful result in all public exams and has acquired position in top 20 of Sylhet education board. It keeps its advancement by maintaining a specific target, short term (weekly) medium term (monthly) and long term etc. The students of the institution take part actively and successfully in the programs organized by Government and local authorities. Reverent Sector Commander is the chairman of the Governing Body (ex –officio) . In all respects, considering over all activities the institution is one of the best in Sylhet division as well as all over Bangladesh. We are committed and determined to uphold this prestigious institution in a sky-catching position through co-operation from all strata of people.

Welcome to Border Guard Public School and College, Sylhet Border Guard Public School & College is situated in a solution environment the Head Quarters of BGB at Akhalia in the Holy land Sylhet. This Institution started its journey with a view to developing the educational development of this region lender the little of Reflex Public School in 1993 with the patronage and management of the then BDR, Sylhet. With the circumlocution of time, it has been named as Border Guard Public School. In 2012 it has been upgraded as college on approval of Ministry of Education and Sylhet Education Board. Enlarged campus well decorated class rooms. shipshape environment, efficient and experienced teachers are always devoted to ensure quality education.
We teach student using advanced learning materials and ensure a congenial learning materials and ensure a congenial environment for connective Practice, regular test, systematic and most importantly science based activities in the classrooms targeting Academic calendar published for each year under the guidance of Minstry of Education, Sylhet Education Board X BGB Sylhet Sector to meet the challenges of 21st century, to bring out proper tatent X tocreat good citizens confidentially and morally and morally.
